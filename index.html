Rules

Belt Tests: belt tests will be held during the last 10 minutes of each music class as well as before and after school on certain days/times. Please find the sign up sheet in the music classroom to sign up for belt tests.

To receive your belt, you must play the piece of music with NO MISTAKES. This means you must play with appropriate tone, correct rhythms and pitches, and breathe in the appropriate places. Each student is allowed ONE restart/do-over for a belt test. If you do not receive your belt after a test, you have to wait until the next week to test. Only ONE test per week is allowed. You may not test for multiple belts in the same week.

If you have received your Black Belt in Recorder Karate, you will be able to move on to Recorder Karate LEVEL 2 (in process).
